Welcome to D2I-Jazz Facebook Tag Extractor Service
The D2I-Jazz Facebook Tag Extractor Service retrieves your Basic Information, Email address, Activities, Birthday, Groups and Likes, Music, TV, Movies, Books, Quotes, Videos, News Feed and Events, from your Facebook account and generates tags of your interest and hobbies. The extracted tags are the only data which are stored about you.
This service adds two Facebook apps to your account, "D2I-VisualLabel" and "D2I-jazz". The first is required for creation of a user account to our service and the second is required for accessing your Facebook account content. You can remove these apps from your Facebook account by using Facebook App Center.
The objective of this application is to collect initial feedback from you on how well the generated labels of interests and hobbies match your expectations.
Instructions
  1. Please sign in with your Facebook account (your account credentials will NOT be stored on our server)
  2. Permissions will be requested twice as described above
  3. A Tag-set of 30 words will appear
  4. Please give your opinion on how useful each word is as a potential label for your hobby and interests
    • A tag may have one of the following states:
    • neutral
    • neutral n-gram
    • accept
    • reject
    • Click a tag once to accept it, twice to reject it, or leave it untouched for neutral state
If you have any questions or there are problems with extracting your tags, email us: cose-pori@tut.fi
Thank You for Your Feedback
